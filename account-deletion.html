Delete Your Warm Check-In Account

We're sorry to see you go! You can delete your Warm Check-In account and all associated data at any time using one of the methods below.

Method 1: Delete Within the App (Recommended)

  1. Open the Warm Check-In app on your device
  2. Navigate to Settings
  3. Tap on Account
  4. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  5. Read the warning and confirm deletion
  6. Your account will be permanently deleted within 30 days

Method 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app or need assistance, you can request account deletion by email:

Email: contact@cupkiki.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Include in your email:

  • Your registered email address
  • Confirmation that you want to delete your account
  • Optional: Reason for deletion (helps us improve)

We will process your request within 7 business days and send you a confirmation email once completed.

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and password
  • Check-in history: All daily check-in records and timestamps
  • Mood tracking data: All mood entries and wellness logs
  • Trusted contacts: Your list of emergency contacts
  • Subscription data: Your payment and subscription information
  • Preferences: All app settings and customizations
  • Notifications: All scheduled reminders and alerts

Data Retention Policy

Most of your data is deleted immediately, but some information may be retained for legal or operational reasons:

  • Transaction records: We retain payment and subscription transaction records for 7 years to comply with tax and financial regulations
  • Aggregated analytics: We may retain anonymized, non-identifiable analytics data that cannot be traced back to you
  • Legal holds: If your data is subject to a legal hold or ongoing investigation, retention may be extended as required by law

Timeline: Your personal data will be deleted within 30 days of your deletion request. After this period, your data cannot be recovered.

⚠️ Important: Account Deletion is Permanent

  • Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered
  • All your check-in history will be lost
  • Your trusted contacts will no longer receive alerts from you
  • If you have an active subscription, it will be cancelled (no refunds for partial periods)
  • You will need to create a new account if you want to use Warm Check-In again

Alternatives to Account Deletion

If you're not ready to permanently delete your account, consider these options:

  • Pause notifications: Turn off check-in reminders in Settings
  • Remove trusted contacts: Clear your contacts list without deleting your account
  • Cancel subscription: Downgrade to the free tier instead of deleting
