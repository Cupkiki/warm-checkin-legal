Privacy Policy for Poop Diary

Last Updated: February 9, 2026

Poop Diary ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

Poop Diary is a privacy-first app. All your data stays on your device. We collect and store the following information locally on your device only:

Data You Provide

  • Bowel movement logs (date, time, Bristol type, color, size, mood)
  • Photos you choose to attach to entries (stored locally and in your device's photo library)
  • Notes and observations you add to entries
  • Food tracking information
  • Health goals and preferences
  • Profile information (name, emoji, for multi-profile feature)

Automatically Collected Data

None. We do not collect analytics, usage data, or any telemetry.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively on your device to:

  • Display your bowel movement history and statistics
  • Generate insights and trends about your digestive health
  • Track progress toward your health goals
  • Sync with Apple Health (iOS only, if you enable it)

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not:

  • Store your data on any servers
  • Upload your data to the cloud
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Track your usage
  • Collect analytics

Your health data never leaves your device except when you explicitly:

  • Export data using the export feature (PDF/CSV/JSON)
  • Share entries or statistics using your device's sharing features
  • Sync with Apple Health (iOS only, requires your permission)
  • Use the Toilet Finder (sends your location to third-party APIs to find nearby restrooms — see "Location" under Permissions)

4. Permissions We Request

Camera (Android & iOS)

Why: To let you take photos of your bowel movements for tracking purposes.
When: Only when you tap the camera button in a new entry.
Storage: Photos are saved locally on your device and to your photo library as backup.

Photo Library (iOS) / Media Library (Android)

Why: To save photos as a backup and allow you to select existing photos.
When: Only when you use the photo feature.
Storage: Photos are saved to your device's photo library.

Apple Health (iOS only)

Why: To sync bowel movement data with Apple Health app (optional).
When: Only if you enable the Health integration feature.
Data: We only read/write bowel movement frequency data.

Location (Optional)

Why: To find nearby public restrooms using the Toilet Finder feature.
When: Only when you open the Toilet Finder and grant permission.
Data sent: Your approximate coordinates are sent to third-party APIs to locate nearby restrooms:

  • Refuge Restrooms API (refugerestrooms.org) — receives your latitude/longitude to return nearby restroom locations
  • OpenStreetMap Overpass API (overpass-api.de) — receives your latitude/longitude to find restroom data in regions not covered by Refuge Restrooms

Storage: Your location is used in real-time only and is not stored or logged by the app.

Third-party privacy policies:
Refuge Restrooms | OpenStreetMap

Notifications (Optional)

Why: To send you reminders to log your bowel movements.
When: Only if you enable daily reminders in settings.
Content: Simple reminder notifications, no personal data included.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to anyone. Your information stays on your device. The only way your data leaves your device is if you choose to:

  • Export it using the export feature
  • Share a screenshot or export file
  • Enable Apple Health sync (data goes to Apple Health, not to us)

6. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

7. Children's Privacy

Poop Diary is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information. All data stays on the device.

8. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: View all your data in the app
  • Delete: Clear all data in Settings → Danger Zone → Clear All Data
  • Export: Export your data anytime using Settings → Export Data

9. Data Security

Your health data is stored using your device's local storage. The app relies on your device's built-in security for protection — including full-disk encryption (enabled by default on modern iOS and Android devices when a passcode is set).

We recommend:

  • Using a device passcode/biometric lock
  • Keeping your device software up to date
  • Regular device backups

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. Medical Disclaimer

Poop Diary is not a medical device. It is for tracking and informational purposes only. Always consult a healthcare provider for medical advice.
