Privacy Policy for Warm Check-In

Effective Date: January 12, 2026

Last Updated: January 12, 2026

Cupkiki LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Warm Check-In mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Create an account: Name, email address, and password
  • Add trusted contacts: Names and phone numbers of your emergency contacts
  • Use premium features: Phone numbers for SMS alert delivery (via Twilio)
  • Contact us: Any information you provide when you reach out to us for support

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Check-in data: Timestamps of your daily check-ins and missed check-ins
  • Mood tracking data: Optional mood entries you choose to record
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage data: App features you use, time spent in the app

1.3 Location Information

We do NOT track your location by default. Location data is only collected if you explicitly enable this feature in the app settings. You can enable or disable location sharing at any time.

1.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your browsing history
  • We do NOT access your contacts without permission
  • We do NOT track your location unless you enable it
  • We do NOT use tracking cookies or advertising identifiers

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide core functionality: Enable daily check-ins and safety monitoring
  • Send notifications: Remind you to check in and alert your trusted contacts when needed
  • Deliver SMS alerts: Send emergency notifications via Twilio (premium feature only)
  • Improve the App: Understand how users interact with features to make improvements
  • Provide customer support: Respond to your questions and troubleshoot issues
  • Ensure security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security issues
  • Legal compliance: Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 With Your Trusted Contacts

We share your check-in status and alerts with the trusted contacts YOU designate in the app. This is the core purpose of the app and only happens when:

  • You miss a check-in and escalation protocols activate
  • You manually trigger an emergency alert
  • You explicitly choose to share mood data with them

3.2 With Service Providers

We share limited information with third-party service providers who help us operate the App:

  • Twilio: We use Twilio to send SMS alerts to your trusted contacts (premium feature only). Twilio receives phone numbers and message content. See Twilio's privacy policy: https://www.twilio.com/legal/privacy
  • Cloud hosting providers: For secure data storage and app functionality

These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and may only use it for providing services to us.

3.3 We Do NOT Sell Your Information

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

  • Legal process (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Requests from law enforcement or government agencies
  • Protection of our rights, property, or safety
  • Emergency situations involving danger of death or serious physical injury

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 How We Protect Your Data

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited employee access to personal data
  • Secure cloud infrastructure

4.2 Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Needed to provide you services
  • Required by law or for legitimate business purposes

When you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we must retain it for legal compliance.

4.3 International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

5. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correct: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications and alerts (though this may limit app functionality)

5.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, you can:

  • Use the settings within the App
  • Email us at: contact@cupkiki.com
  • Delete your account directly in the app settings

We will respond to your request within 30 days.

5.3 Marketing Communications

We do not send marketing emails. You will only receive:

  • Check-in reminders (core app function)
  • Emergency alerts (as configured)
  • Important service updates
  • Support responses

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at contact@cupkiki.com and we will delete such information.
